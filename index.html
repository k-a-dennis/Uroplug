Our Mission

Empowering patients
Improving lives

Chronic urinary incontinence affects hundreds of millions worldwide. Current treatments often fall short, burdening patients with inconvenient implementation, high costs, and limited efficacy. Uroplug exists to change that.

Why Uroplug

backed by science

Clinically Validated

Rigorous clinical trials designed to meet FDA clearance standards, with peer-reviewed data supporting safety and efficacy.

At-Home Convenience

A compact, intuitive device that patients use in the comfort of their own home — no clinic visits, no downtime, no disruption.

Long-Term Relief

Designed to address the root mechanisms of chronic conditions — not just mask symptoms — for sustained therapeutic benefit.

Precision engineering meets patient comfort
