IP Strategy

Building a defensible moat

Our intellectual property strategy is designed to create broad protection across four key domains: core device architecture, control and dosimetry systems, materials and biointerface design, and software/AI-driven treatment protocols.

We file continuation and divisional applications to extend coverage as the technology evolves, and we pursue international filings in key markets aligned with our commercial strategy.

All IP is fully owned by Uroplug, Inc. with clean chain-of-title documentation.
